Hard Refresh Instructions

To clear browser cache and see the fixed version:

  1. Chrome/Edge: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows)
  2. Firefox: Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+F5 (Windows)
  3. Safari: Cmd+Option+R (Mac)

Or open DevTools and right-click the refresh button, then select "Empty Cache and Hard Reload"
